Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich, savory meat sauce. This comfort food favorite is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. Simple to prepare, but packed with flavor.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and cook until soft.
  3. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute, then stir in the ground beef. Cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce, season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 25–30 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti, garnish with fresh basil, and enjoy!

Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering. You can also substitute ground beef with ground turkey for a leaner option.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)
